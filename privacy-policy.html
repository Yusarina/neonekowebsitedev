Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 01/08/2024

This Privacy Policy outlines how Neoneko ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our websites (neoneko.xyz and catsblenderplugin.xyz) and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data practices.

Information We Collect

We do not intentionally collect any personal data from our users. However, our web hosting service and content delivery network may automatically collect and store the following information:

  • IP Address: Your IP address may be logged by our web server and CDN for security and operational purposes.
  • Browser Type: Information about the type of browser and operating system you use may be collected to optimize your experience on our website.

Browser Storage

We use browser storage to remember your theme preference. This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

Use of Content Delivery Network (CDN)

We use Bunny CDN to serve files and images to our users efficiently. Bunny CDN may collect and process certain user data, such as IP addresses and user agents, for the purpose of delivering content and improving service performance. For more information on how Bunny CDN handles data, please refer to their privacy policy.

How We Use Information

The limited information collected is used solely for the purpose of maintaining and improving our website's functionality, security, and content delivery. We do not use this information for marketing or tracking purposes.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, such as our CDN provider, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of the limited information collected.

Your Rights

While we don't collect personal data, you have the right to inquire about any information we might have. If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you may have additional rights under the GDPR.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

Governing Law
